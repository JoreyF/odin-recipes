Chicken Alfredo

Finished dish of perfectly made chicken alfredo

This recipe I will show you today will allow you to cook the best dish of chicken alfredo. This dish is great for big meals with the family since it is easy to make in bulk.

In this article I will show you how to cook this step by step!

Ingredients
  • 8 oz fettuccine noodles,cooked according to package directions
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 cups grated Parmesean

    • Melt butter in saucepan over low heat. Sauta garlic for about 30 seconds, or until fragrant. Stir in cream. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Add the Parmesan and stir until melted. Thin with pasta water if necessary.

    Drain the pasta and immediately pour into serving bowl. Pour sauce over and toss to combine.
